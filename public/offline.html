🚨 If You're Being Detained

  • "I am exercising my right to remain silent."
  • "I do not consent to a search."
  • "Am I free to go?"

📋 Your Rights (Quick Reference)

  • 1st Amendment: You can film police in public.
  • 4th Amendment: No unreasonable searches without a warrant.
  • 5th Amendment: You have the right to remain silent.
  • 6th Amendment: You have the right to an attorney.
  • 14th Amendment: Equal protection under the law.
